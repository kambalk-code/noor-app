Privacy Policy — Effective Date: March 24, 2026
At Noor, we take your privacy seriously — especially given the personal and religious nature of the information you share with us. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, who we share it with, and the rights you have over your information. Please read it carefully before using our app.

1. Who We Are

Noor ("we," "us," or "our") is a Muslim matrimony application designed to help Muslims find a spouse in a halal, respectful environment. Our app is available globally, including in the United States and all countries worldwide.

For any privacy-related questions, you may contact us at the email address listed at the bottom of this policy.

2. Information We Collect

Information You Provide Directly

  • Account Information: First name, date of birth, gender, and email address
  • Profile Information: Photos, ethnicity/background, country and city of origin, current location, religious practice level, sect, prayer habits, hijab/beard status, marital status, education, profession, and personal bio
  • Preferences: Who you are looking for, marriage timeline, and whether you want children
  • Communications: Messages you send to other users through the app
  • Payment Information: Credit/debit card details when subscribing to Noor Gold ($5/month). Card data is processed by our payment provider and is not stored on our servers

Information Collected Automatically

  • Device type, operating system, and app version
  • IP address and approximate location
  • Usage data: which features you use, how long you use the app, swipe activity
  • Log data: crash reports, performance data

Sensitive Information

☽ Noor collects sensitive personal data including religious beliefs, religious practice, and ethnicity. We collect this only to help you find a compatible spouse. You may choose what to share and what to keep private on your profile.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Create and maintain your account and profile
  • Show your profile to other users and show you potential matches
  • Enable messaging between matched users
  • Process your Noor Gold subscription payment
  • Send you notifications about matches, messages, and app updates
  • Improve and personalize your experience on the app
  • Detect and prevent fraud, abuse, and violations of our Terms of Service
  • Comply with legal obligations

We do not use your data to serve third-party advertisements. Noor is ad-free.

4. How We Share Your Information

With Other Users

Your profile information — including your name, age, location, photos, and religious details you choose to share — is visible to other Noor users who match your search criteria.

With Service Providers

We share data with trusted third-party providers who help us operate the app, including:

  • Payment processors (for Noor Gold subscriptions)
  • Cloud hosting and storage providers
  • Analytics providers (to improve app performance)
  • Customer support tools

All service providers are contractually bound to handle your data securely and only for the purposes we specify.

Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law, court order, or government authority, or to protect the rights, property, or safety of Noor, our users, or the public.

We Never Sell Your Data

🔒 We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to any third party for their own marketing purposes.

5. Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, we will delete your profile and personal data within 30 days, except where we are required to retain it for legal compliance purposes.

Messages exchanged with other users may be retained for up to 90 days after deletion for safety and abuse-prevention purposes.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your country of residence, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: Request a copy of the data we hold about you
  • Correction: Request that we correct inaccurate data
  • Deletion: Request that we delete your account and personal data
  • Portability: Request your data in a portable format
  • Objection: Object to certain types of processing
  • Restriction: Request that we limit how we use your data
  • Withdraw Consent: Where processing is based on consent, withdraw it at any time

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the email address below. We will respond within 30 days.

7. Children's Privacy

Noor is intended for users who are 18 years of age or older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 18. If we become aware that a user is under 18, we will immediately delete their account and associated data.

If you believe a minor has registered on our platform, please contact us immediately.

8. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal data, including:

  • Encryption of data in transit (TLS/SSL)
  • Encryption of data at rest
  • Secure payment processing through certified payment providers
  • Regular security audits and vulnerability testing
  • Access controls limiting who within our team can access user data

No system is 100% secure. If you believe your account has been compromised, please contact us immediately.

9. International Data Transfers

Noor operates globally, including in the United States and countries worldwide. Your data may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own. We take appropriate safeguards to ensure your data is protected in accordance with this policy regardless of where it is processed.

10. Subscriptions & Payments

Noor Gold is offered as a monthly subscription at $5 per month. Payment is processed through our third-party payment provider. You may cancel your subscription at any time through your account settings or by contacting us. Cancellation takes effect at the end of the current billing period.

We do not store your full card number, CVV, or expiry date on our servers. All payment data is handled by our PCI-DSS compliant payment processor.

11. Wali (Guardian) Feature

Noor offers an optional Wali (guardian) feature that allows a trusted family member or guardian to be included in conversations. If a user invites a Wali, that person will have access to the conversation thread. Users are responsible for obtaining the Wali's consent before adding them to the app.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we make significant changes, we will notify you via email or an in-app notification at least 14 days before the changes take effect. Your continued use of Noor after that date constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

The date at the top of this document reflects the most recent update.
